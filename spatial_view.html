Trend in Speed Violations across Locations (2015 - 2021)

Below Linked Map and Bar Chart Shows the Trend in Speed Violations Across Different Locations Over the Years. The Map Shows Cluster with Number of Locations. The Bar Chart Shows the Chicago Trend Over the Years at Start, but When Zommed in on Clusters and Selected a Locations Shows that Particular Locations Trend.
